Who am I?

I'm a Learning Technologist with a passion for building clean, efficient content. My journey into coding began during the pandemic when I made a career switch from Human Resources. Since then, I haven't looked back.

My experiences span both creative and technical fields, from digital transformation to designing and developing accessible programmes. Accessibility is always at the forefront of my work.

As a self-motivated learner, I thrive in dynamic environments and I am always eager to expand my knowledge in various technologies.

Fun fact: I love solving puzzles, tackling escape rooms, and mastering Rubik's cubes. In a previous chapter of my life, I worked in Tenerife doing conservation work.
